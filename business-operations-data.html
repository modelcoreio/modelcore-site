Common questions

The details, up front.

Why do AI labs want our data?

Frontier models are increasingly trained to work inside companies, using real tools, workflows, and decisions. Public and synthetic data cannot replicate that. Your operational data captures how organizations actually run, which is exactly what labs need to build AI that handles real enterprise tasks.

How is compensation calculated?

Compensation is based on data volume, the systems you include, and the depth and uniqueness of the data. Broader scope generally means a higher payout. We give you an estimate before anything moves.

Is there any cost to us?

No. ModelCore Data covers all costs of extraction, anonymization, and transfer. You receive compensation with no upfront cost, setup fee, or deduction.

Who sees our raw data?

The extraction and anonymization process is automated. Sensitive identifiers are removed before any buyer sees the data, and no buyer or third party ever receives your raw content. All access is logged and governed by strict internal controls.

Do we keep ownership of our data?

Yes. You license your data on defined terms; you are not selling your company or handing over ownership. You can set exclusions, and raw data is never resold.

When do we get paid?

Payment is issued by wire once the anonymized dataset is delivered to the buyer, typically within a few weeks of a signed agreement, depending on scope and processing time.
